FAQ

Common questions

When is ConnectPaw launching?+

We're targeting MVP launch in August 2026. The product is actively being built right now. Join the waitlist to be notified first and secure early access.

What's the difference between Basic Care and Advanced Care?+

Basic Care is delivered by vet professionals (nurses or techs) via live video — they triage your pet's symptoms and tell you the next step. Advanced Care involves an AVS-licensed vet doctor via live video who can formally diagnose and prescribe — activated when Basic Care determines it's clinically necessary.

Is ConnectPaw legal in Singapore?+

Yes. ConnectPaw operates in full compliance with the AVS (Animal & Veterinary Service) guidelines and the Vet Practice Bill. All consults are logged, vet credentials are verified, and we maintain clear boundaries between teleadvice/teletriage and telemedicine that requires a Veterinary-Client-Patient Relationship (VCPR).

Are consultations done by video or text?+

Live video — never text chat. Our vets need to actually see your pet to assess symptoms accurately. It's the closest thing to a clinic visit, without the drive.

What happens if my pet needs emergency care?+

If our vet professional determines your pet needs immediate physical emergency care, they'll direct you to the nearest emergency clinic right away — and your ConnectPaw consult fee is waived. Your pet's safety always comes first.

Will it replace my regular vet?+

No — we're complementary, not competitive. You can link your existing clinic vet to your ConnectPaw account to maintain continuity of care, especially for prescriptions and chronic conditions.

Do you offer plans for businesses, clinics, or insurers?+

Yes. We work with employers including pet healthcare in their benefits, vet clinics extending post-discharge support, and insurers exploring a white-label telehealth layer. Reach out via the form below.
